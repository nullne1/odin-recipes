Egg Fried Rice

A Dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten as a main dish or as an accompaniment to another dish.

Ingredients

  • Day old rice
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Carrots
  • Frozen Peas
  • Soy Sauce
  1. Cook eggs and set aside
  2. Sautee garlic and onions
  3. Put frozen peas and carrots in
  4. Put rice in
  5. Season with soy sauce
